Simple CRM is a browser-based customer relationship management application that operates entirely on the client-side without requiring a backend server or database. FLAMES Game

This game is played to predict the future relationship between a person and their crush (or celebrity crush). If a player does not achieve the desired outcome, they may retry with different variations of their names to influence the outcome. The reactions to the game's outcome may reveal a player's true feelings toward the person they are paired with. The game, however, does not accurately predict the true relationship between any two persons.
